SeeHealth

Duration: Mar. 2023 ~ Jun. 2023
Course Project for 'Capstone Design Project 1' @ KNU

This IoT device was developed with consideration for visually impaired individuals who can collect their own samples using a self-testing kit but cannot determine the results due to their lack of vision. After collecting the sample using a self-testing kit, placing it on the device initiates the photo capture process. Through a deep learning network, the IoT device, called SeeHealth, interprets the results as either 'positive,' 'negative,' or 'undetected' and communicates this information to the user through voice.
